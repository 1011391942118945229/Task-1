Welcome to Amazon

Amazon is one of the largest and most influential technology companies in the world, originally founded by Jeff Bezos in 1994 as an online bookstore. Since then, it has evolved into a global e-commerce giant, offering millions of products ranging from electronics and clothing to groceries and digital services. Beyond retail, Amazon has become a major player in cloud computing through its Amazon Web Services (AWS) division.
